Breathing Exercises

Breathing exercises are...

We're going to be using the box method, which consists of four steps:

  1. Breathe in slowly for four seconds.
  2. Hold for four seconds.
  3. Breathe out slowly for four seconds.
  4. Rest and repeat.

The four different steps of the method are just like the four different sides of a box!
